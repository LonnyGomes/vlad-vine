# Share Your Images

Help document Bulbutin's adventures around the world! Add your photos to the feed by uploading them to this folder and creating a pull request.

## Requirements

> [!important]
> **Your photos must contain GPS coordinates (EXIF metadata)** so we can track where Bulbutin has been adventuring!


## How to Contribute

1. **Add your photos** - Upload the images [here](https://github.com/LonnyGomes/bulbutin/tree/main/packages/parse-photos/images)
3. **Create a pull request** - Submit your photos for review
4. **Wait for merge** - Once approved, your photos will appear in Bulbutin's feed!
